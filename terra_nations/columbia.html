Columbia

About

Columbia, used to be known as he Columbian Union, is a democratic nation in Terra. It is based on the country of United States of America, and it is the only democratic nation in Terra. As such, it is governed by three branches in the government: executive, legislative, and judiciary. Columbia is led by a president and is made out of various federal states.

Much like the US, Columbia is a melting pot of culture and is the youngest nation in Terra. It is also a major center of technology, with many technological advancements being founded in Columbia. The nation is also known for its focus on equality, treating commonly discriminated minorities such as the Sarkaz and infected much better than other nations.

Columbia is home to many huge corporations and a huge military. Through this, Columbia is a major dominant power in Terra, and has become a expansionist superpower.
