Quick Summary

Higashi

A nation with incredibly vibrant and colorful culture. Its video games, manga, and anime have rocked the world and influenced many Terran youths.

About

Higashi is a nation in Terra that is based off Japan. Unlike its real-world counterparts, Yan and Higashi have strong diplomatic ties and have exchanged cultures extensively. It was once ruled by a single powerful clan, but internal conflicts led to the country be divided into the Northern Court and the Souther Court, ruled by Kougon and Mitsumoto respectively.

Higashi is controlled by two clans, known as the Kougon and the Mitsumoto. It is home to a large population of Aegir, and also houses all Onis.
