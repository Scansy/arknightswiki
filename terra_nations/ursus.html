Ursus

A gigantic and militaristic country in Terra. Infamous for their expansionistic tendencies and extremely poor treatment to the infected, Ursus has made quite a name in the world stage.

About

Ursus, formally known as Empire of Ursus and formerly known as Hippogryph Empire, is a nation in Terra based on Russia. It is the largest nation in Terra and is ruled by an emperor known as the Tsar.

Ursus is known for their military prowess and homogenous population, mostly inhabited by the Ursine race. The nation is facing a power struggle between its aristocrats and military leaders.

Ursus is quite infamous in Terra's politics, known for being extremely militaristic and xenophobic. Ursus also treats their infected very poorly, sending them to mining camps in the frigid Northern Tundra. These laborers are forced to work until they die from oripathy or for the overseers and guards' entertainment.
