About

Sargon, formerlly the Sargon Empire, is a nation in Arknights that is based on the real world region of the middle east. The nation is comprised of both dry deserts and wet, lush tropical rainforests. Despite being one of the oldest an politically influential nation in Terra, it is currently facing hardships and separatism.

The nation is led by a king called "Shahanshah" that resides in "the Golden City". However, the shahanshah is practically powerless and the nation is led by regional warlords and clan leaders instead. The arid deserts of Sargon is the center of many conflicts and where many mercenaries are hired to do skirmishes.

The jungles of Sargon is primarily inhabited by primitives tribes of the Archosaurian and Pythian races. They seldom have contact to the outside world, but this leads to a very unique culture that is vastly different from the rest of Sargon.
