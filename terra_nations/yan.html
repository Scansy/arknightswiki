Yan

A great and prosperous nation in Yan. It's unique govermental system is unlike no other, and with its rich culture and deep history, Yan is a deeply influential nation in Terra.

About

Yan, formally known as Great Yan, is a nation in Terra that is based on China. It is an ancient nation that has existed for many centuries and is led by an imperial monarchy.

Yan is a big and prosperous country, with a robust economy and beautiful landscapes. Yan, unlike many nations in Terra, shows great tolerance to its racial diversity, sometimes even endorsing minorities. However, it still treats the infected harshly.

Yan's cities are unique in the sense that each has their own specific administrative roles. It's largest city, Lungmen, acts as an economic zone while Yumen is governed by the military.
